# New Identity Hub – Women Empowerment Website

## Live Demo
https://the-new-identity-hub.vercel.app/

## Tagline
Empowering Women Beyond Four Walls

## Project Overview
New Identity Hub is a modern multi-page website designed for a women empowerment initiative focused on helping homemakers rediscover their confidence, develop skills, and create financial independence.  

The website provides information about mindset development, entrepreneurship opportunities, community support, and structured learning programs for women who want to transform their personal and professional identity.

## Pages Included
- Home – The New Identity Hub
- About Us – Philosophy, Mission, Vision
- Self Discovery Suite (Mindset & Confidence)
- Entrepreneurship & Branding Lab
- Home & Harmony
- The Sisterhood – Community Forum
- Courses & Registration
